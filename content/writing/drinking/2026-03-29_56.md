title: Day 56
---

Today I wanted to drink. I was in an aisle full of booze and I looked at it real long and hard. I didn't buy anything. I didn't drink anything. I'm still tempted. Even though I can resist it. I felt good about having the strength of resolve to not fall to it. I wonder what it would be like if I was back in Japan. If I were living there. I feel like I would still be drinking. Maybe I wouldn't. Maybe I'd be having mugicha instead.

It's hard to tell. I do miss Japan so much. I miss going to work. I miss going around town. I miss having my two storey apartment. I miss using the language every day. I miss the food. I miss the people. I miss the scenery. I miss the architecture. I miss my friends. I miss the people I met. I miss having something that I did every day. I miss the confectionary. I miss how cheap everything was. 

I could go on longer. I am not happy where I am. I'm happy using the law, and I'd like a higher salary if and when I go back. That road does seem somewhat fraught. Maybe I might have a chance. Realistically I don't have a snowball's chance in hell. Realistically I won't make it. I won't make it back. I won't make it there. I won't make it anywhere. I don't know what's going to happen to me. I need to make peace with that.
