title: Day 45

Today I wanted to drink. This week has been one of the worst. My work ethic has become untennable. Nothing is working out right and it's all breaking down again. Two Friday 13ths in one year. Me trying to blame my failings on the black star. To have my accountability washed away by fate. It was fated I would suffer. It was fated that I would be crushed. Broken apart. Ground into meat. 

I think about putting my arm into some big machine sometimes. Always my right arm. Feeling the skin peel away. Feeling the nerves serge pain up through them, as they're severed and broken beyond repair. The meat blitzed and minced. Just like any other meat. Then the bone, it lasts a second longer before being pulverised into white mist. Then it's gone. Part of my body is gone. It happened in an instant. It will never be the same. 

Brain damage. Caused by alcohol. Caused by stress and pain. The standard profile of injury incurred in a single day. I wonder if its the same. Something that was there, broken and destroyed in an instant. Destruction is so much more quick than building. Just let it all go. Let it all get blasted apart. Matter cannot be created or destroyed but the particular arrangements can be. The use can be broken apart. To the point where it's meaningless atoms spread across my arms. Relationships. Neurons. Limbs. All gone in an instant. A flash in the pan. Like the rest of us. Returned as calcium deposits six feet under.
