title: Day 57

Today I wanted to drink. I remembered a specific brand of Japanese sake I used to drink and I missed the taste of it. To tell the truth I miss the effect of it too. I miss dry alcohol. I miss that sensation of having it burn the back of your throat when it's strong. I miss the feeling of the numbness setting in. The closest I get nowadays is when I smoke. That first drag. Feeling the nicotine reach my brain. The way it all seems to relax all at once. Bad for health but good for staying alive.

That's the circumstance of addiction ultimately. It is a way of making it so you can force your shit together. You don't have time for introspection. You don't have time to fix it. You don't even know how to fix it. It's treating the symptom and leaving the disease festering away. Something so rotten and broken that there's no real way of knowing how to fix it. 

No witness marks. Clockwork shaved away and broken. Best you can do is oil the gears and pray. That's it. Try and glue the cracks together knowing that the wood glue won't hold. And then you're surrounded by empty glue bottles and unrolled tape. It's still ticking. How much longer will it tick. How much longer before the layers of varnish and oil invite the final obliviating flame. Time will tell. Ticking away. 

Until the broken thing is finally brought its quietus.
