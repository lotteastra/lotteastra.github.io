Today I didn't want to drink. I didn't think about it too much. It feels natural. Though to counter drinking never felt natural in the first place. It was someething I did in response to stimuli. Now I smoke and I cry. I don't smoke that much. It doesn't grip the same part of my mind. It's strange how easily I can shake one substance but not the other. Part of me debates going back. Thinks that it's been long enough. I wonder if any amount of time without alcohol will be enough for me. Someday maybe. I like the taste. 

I'm so tired today. It's hard to keep my thoughts together in the first place. Like I'm holding a bunch of stuff and struggling not to drop it. Except it's my thoughts. Like there's not enough strength in my brain to even coherently maintain a thought. I want to lie down and rest and rest and sleep and sleep. I am ever so tired.
