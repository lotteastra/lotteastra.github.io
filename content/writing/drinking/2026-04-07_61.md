Today did not particularly feel like drinking. I forgot about it. I didn't want to smoke either. I studied. Less than I should have. More than I otherwise would. I didn't eat much today. I ate two doughnuts I had laying around and some nutella. It probably was close to enough calories. Taking these things into me. Making them part of me. Same thing as anything else. You jam rocks together. Stick them there and it all becomes one unit. 

Do you ever think of the mass of everything the average human has eaten. One of my favourite exihibits is in the British museum. It is a huge rug filled with all the medication a person will ever take in someone's life. Even that mass of pills and injections. It wouldn't ammount to more than a loaf of bread. How many loaves of bread have you eaten. Pile it up. Every rice grain. Every 2 litre soda. I wonder if it could fill up a whole room. A whole house. I wonder. I wonder which foods would have been the best. Which ones I have the fondest memories of. There are places I could call the best in each of their class.

I remember whenever I made plans to kill myself always, forever, at the top of the list was I wouldn't be able to eat anymore. Food is life. Food makes me want to live. I'm another animal. Looking for a bone.
