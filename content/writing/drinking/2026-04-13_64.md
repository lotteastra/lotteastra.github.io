title: Day 64
---

Today I wanted to drink. It was passing. I consider myself to have done rather well with myself in the circumstances I find myself wrapped around. Caught in a gravitational pull of stress. I can feel my limbs stretching out. The event horizon is not too far away now. Break my bones. They weren't doing anybody any good. Find everything you hold dear. Everything you hoped for. Everything you wished would some day be yours. Imagine it rotting away. You'll never be able to have what you want. It will always be beyond your grasp. Distract. Evade. It's wasted effort. It's too close now. You'll die. You'll surely die. 

I'm nearly 26 and the stress of that weighs on me. I'm at an age that could no longer truely be considered young. The sum total of my achievements is nil. It is a litany of pain and failure. Tourment and haphazard half hearted thrusts that go nowhere. Mean nothing. I have tried so hard to take hold of what I want. I keep on trying. Keep on struggling. Why do I keep doing this to myself. What's the point. What was the point in the first place. It was easier to rot. Once you're caught in the grasp of something like that. A small mistake tumbling through. A landslide. You can't break away. All you can do is wait for the rock to crack your skull open. Like an egg. Watch your yolk run. Maybe something will eat it. Get some use out of what once was you.
