title: Day 51
---

Today I wanted to drink. I walked past several pubs and wished to go in. To fill my belly and obliviate my mind. Once again it has been another stressful week. The stress churning around, churning away. Stones scraping the inside of my stomach digesting me alive. That's why I wanted to drink. Even though my sleep has improved immeasurably. To tell the truth sleep was the one particular issue I had when I was drinking. 

I can't help but look back at my time drinking as that of a happier time. One where I could drink my issues away. Where I wouldn't be tempted. Perhaps it is more a question or a relationship to the fact that the factual matrix of my life has degraded moreso than it had before. I was more independent. I was in a place I enjoyed. I was stressed but the stress felt different. I had hope.

At that point in time I still had a chance of my most preferred option. Which then disappeared as time went on. The first death was the death of that hope. It will return someday. I hope it will. Until then I am left in darkness. The eclipse stretching on and on. Until I am able to relight my sun. To find that hope again and enjoy those bright days anew. Nothing will last forever. We won't and this moment won't. Perhaps one day I may even not want to drink anymore.
