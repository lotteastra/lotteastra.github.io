Today I wanted to drink. I got angry. I lost my nerve. My temper. I'm in a space between caring and not caring. Trying to be easy going while I feel easily annoyed. I'm eating myself. My mood half processed into sludge. The sadnest cafeteria chicken nuggets. I don't know how to process anything. I just repackage the rot. I eat it over and over again. It makes me sick. It makes me angry. It hurts. It bleeds. I want it to stop. I cut my hands repurposing it over and over. 

I remember it. Trace my life in the scars. There's a pattern there if you know how to look. Each constillation, the jagged edge of each memory. Corrupted and leaking. Electrical signals misfiring. A misery that is only privy to someone as stupid as I. It smells. I smell. I will always smell. I have smelled. It clings to me, the smell. It is me. I will never be rid of it. 

Eat my brain and maybe you'll taste my memory. Fat and DNA carrying the last will and testament to be transmitted. Final words said through another mouth. The last sound of glass cracking and shattering into something meaningless, deadly and sharp. Say the words that I taught you to say. See the world how I taught you to see it. I live on, a parasite in you. One that will never be known. An influence so subtle that it could never fill a full portrait. But maybe the sillhouette lives on. Darkness lingering in light.
