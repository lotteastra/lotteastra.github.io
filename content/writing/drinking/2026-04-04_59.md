Today I wanted to drink. I wanted to lose myself in that again. I smoked which took the edge off at least. It's time to study and study hard. Not give into despair or procrastination. Another step on the long, long, march. It does get tiring. The ingnition key chipped. Barely enough to twist the gears. To bring cylanders to life. Half are cracked. Performance loss.

I don't know how I'll get through. People have called me a strong person. I'm not sure if that's true. I'm not really sure what strength means. To call someone a strong person. It means to survive. To get up that hill. To go off road. Again and again. It's not something chosen. You survive because you had no choice to. You leave it broken. There are dents throughout. You won't be the same again.

Then the next time you go through it again. Same agnozing steps. You don't know. It's a gamble if this will be where you give out. It's stupid. I'm so stupid. It's not anything. I should be able to just deal with this. All this sentimental bullshit about being broken. About being strong. Just fucking stop. All you were ever good at was feeling pity for yourself.
