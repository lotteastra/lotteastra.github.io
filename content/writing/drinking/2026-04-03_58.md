title: Day 58
---

Today I wanted to drink. Another idle ache at the back of my mind. Goading me to take a drink. I will refrain, my continued refrain. When I listen to it's siren song trying to siphon my consciousness past the lip of the can. Through the bottle. Dissolved like bacteria. Alcohol kills its maker after all. Once the concentration becomes too high in fermentation the yeast all dies off. The thing they used to protect themselves burning them away. All that they were reduced to muck, at the bottom.

It the time of assize. I will be judged. I will be found wanting. I am already burning away my ticket. It's smouldering remains in my hand. It's too far away. It was always too remote. The hammer will come down. Sentenced to sludge. To become meaningless. To have all meaning burned away. For what did I aim to come here. For what do I toil. Toil begeting more toil. Until I burn myself away. It's all circular. One big dumb circle. Before you even know it you feel the centrifugal force. You'll never escape it. Whatever you leave will spin and spin. All over again. Forever and ever.
