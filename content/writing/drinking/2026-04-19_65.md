Today I wanted to  drink. I wanted to relax. I've been procrastinating and burned out. I'm another year older. I don't like how old I am. This blur of life since I turned 18 or so. Years passing in front of my eyes. I've never had a year without wanting to kill myself. I keep on trying to push things away. Defer the emotional reaction. Defer the pain. Let the negative image of it be the measure of your thoughts. The little spots of darkness that you can fit your entire consciousness into. 

It's a sum. Adding up and compounding interest that is never going to be repayable. Mistakes burning my hands away. Confined thoughts that threaten to bleed into the sum. To make it payable. Ripping it all apart. Sometimes I cry without a cause. Sometimes I scream for help without a cause. The stronger I am told I am, the more broken I feel. The more I feel a broken fuse at the back of my brain. This is something that will never be healed. There is no strength, no honour. Just pain. Stress. 

Kidneys hurting cortisol pumping. Forever and ever. Till I breathe my last burnt out lightbulb broken glass. No current able to pass no more through here. The wires are too frayed to conduct. There's too much resistance. Broken and remade, broken and remade. Over and over. It hurts so bad.
