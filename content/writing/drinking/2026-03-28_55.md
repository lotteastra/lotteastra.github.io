Today I did not want to drink. I think I feel exhausted and not in the fun way. In the passing out kind of way. Eyes forced to close. Nothing capable of keeping me awake. Having had an erratic sleep schedule for some weeks now it is taking it's toll. I don't like inflicting issues like this on myself. For one it's the kind of problem that's not worth sympathy. It is entirely self inflicted. It reflects my pathetic lifestyle. I had wanted to have a normal sleep schedule. I had one in the past. When I lived in Japan. It was the place that made me happy.

I miss it. I keep on trying to scheme ways to get over there. I know it's not going to happen. It's so incredibly unlikely and so very restrictive. The professional pathways are a mess. Time consuming too. I hate how much time I have to burn on the time before. I want to stop with the foreplay. The miserable foreplay that keeps on dragging on and on and on. It's all so tiresome. Stuck in a waiting room reading the same bad magazines. While the doctor sees patient after patient. I suppose that's what I get. Starting behind, burning years of my life. The only mistake you can make, is staying in the same place. Not trying to movme forward. Or else you will find the options falling away. Your future burned by inaction.
