---
title: Kraken
---

I know I should hate her. Everything about her brings ruin. Brings pain. It was a mistake. It was a mistake from the beginning. It felt good. Falling into it. I didn't notice the teeth of those jaws as they closed around me. Only the warmth of an open mouth inviting me in. The only place I had ever been invited into. A person like me. She has a hold of me now. She knows she does. She knows the pain she inflicts. I'm nothing to her. Nothing more than a stress ball. Squeeze my guts out. Popped right from under my skin. The worst part is I won't even hate her for it. That part of me long since removed. Dulled and deformed. Do I want that part of me back. I don't know. I'm not good at knowing things. If I was I never would've ended up here. Is that true? She told me what to do, she opened her mouth, and I followed her directions, down, down, deep. That's where you're supposed to go. How was I supposed to know that wasn't what she wanted. That she would spit me out. Throw me away. Use me. Crush me. Over and over. Under her weight. I gotta get out of here. I can't. It's a part of me already. Breaking the will to run. I have legs. I won't ever run. Not from her.
