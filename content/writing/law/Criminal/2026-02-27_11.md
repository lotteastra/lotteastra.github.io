title: Criminal Law Non-Fatal Offences Against the Person Notes
---

Assault is contained within the Offences Against the Persons Act 1861

Section 39 Assault/Battery

R v. Thomas -> Touching a girl's skirt was battery

R(Kracher) v. Leicester Magistrates' Court -> D charged with assault by battery because the court couldn't find enough evidence to prove that they hit someone but they did find enough that they had threatened that person. He was charged with battery and so could not ultimately be tried for common assault. 

R(Ward) v. Black Country Magistrates Court. D charged with common assault, but claimed that he had only done battery
