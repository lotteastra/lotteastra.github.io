---
title: Overall Criminal Checklist
---

1. Read the facts
2. Identify possible offences
3. Apply the facts to the actus reus
    a. Examine the facts of the case
    b. Apply the facts to the elements of the actus reus
    c. If you can connect the facts to then proceed
    d. Consider causation if a results based crime
    e. Consider omissions liability so long as the offence can be committed by ommision
    f. If the elements are all satisfied by the facts then proceed
    g. Cases are used to apply the facts to the elements
4. Apply the facts to the mens rea
    a. Examine the facts of the case
    b. Apply the facts to each element of the mens rea applying as closely as possible to idenfity where X meets Y
    c. If you can connect the facts then proceed
    d. Consider temporal coincidence if an issue
    e. Consider any other issues here
    f. If the elements are all satisfied by the facts then proceed
    g. Cases are used to apply the facts to the elements
5. Examine any possible defenses
    a. Examine the facts of the case
    b. Examine possible defenses that may arise from the facts
    c. Apply the defenses to the case
    d. Proceed to next offence/defendant
