---
title: Judicial Review Checklist
---

1. Narrow vires? (Beyond the powers)
2. Broad vires? (Fettered discretion, abandonment of discretion, abuse of discretion (improper purpose, irrelevant considerations))
3. Proceedural imporpriety? (Natural justice concerns, right to be heard, right to be notified)
4. Proceedural impropriety optional extras? (Legitimate expectations, right to reasons not sufficient by itself, right to a hearing, right to cross examine/legal representation) all contingent on the nature of the issue
5. Bias? (direct, appearance and apparent, first two automatic, last non-automatic)
6. Unreasonableness/Irrationality (Proportionality questions? But generally a different test following Keyu)
